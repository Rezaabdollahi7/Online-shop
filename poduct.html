Introduction

Apple's MacBook laptops to date have been able to perform well and acceptable performance without any additional definitions. One of the powerful laptops in this series is the MacBook MK193 2021. The laptop is equipped with a 16.2 -inch display with a resolution of 3456 × 3456 pixels of the Liquid Retina XDR, which is capable of displaying 254 pixels per inch. We have to say that, given the specifications, we have a powerful and high quality display. But the powerful features of this screen are not over and you should know that it is capable of delivering an update rate of 120 Hz, which brings you a smooth screen without any lag. But undoubtedly the main feature of Apple's series laptops is the processors intended. The MacBook MK193 2021 is also equipped with a powerful Apple M1 Pro processor, which is capable of providing excellent performance in executing heavy software. The 2.1kg weight of this laptop also has no problem with it, and the compact size of the MacBook Pro MK193 2021 does not occupy much space.

Overview

  • MacBook Pro 2021 has a new design. The screen of this MacBook is designed in 2 sizes of 14 inches and 16 inches, and their size has been slightly increased. The dimensions of the 14-inch model are 1.55cm x 31.26cm x 22.12cm, which is slightly larger than the MacBook Pro M1 (13-inch) which measures 1.56cm x 30.41cm x 21.24cm. The 16-inch MacBook Pro is also designed in the size of 1.62cm x 35.79cm x 24.59cm. The weight of the 16-inch model is about 0.2 kg and the 14-inch model is 1.6 kg.

  • Currently, the 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pros have a 1080p FaceTime HD camera. This new camera doubles image resolution and dramatically improves low-light performance. It can be said that this is the same camera used in the iMac M1, which was presented in 2021. Apple has been able to improve the sound quality and audio features of its new device by using studio-quality mics. In this way, it reduces thermal noise and creates clearer contacts. Apple has also added a six-speaker audio system to the 2021 MacBook, which features two tweeters and four force-cancelling woofers. Apple has announced that this will result in 80% more bass. The audio system of the new MacBooks also supports spatial audio for a 3D listening experience.

  • The 2021 MacBook Pro was released on October 18 in 14.2-inch and 16.2-inch sizes, featuring mini-LED displays, more ports, up to 64GB of storage, and Apple's more powerful chips, the M1 Pro and M1 Max. . In short, we can say that they are the best MacBook Pro models introduced to date.
